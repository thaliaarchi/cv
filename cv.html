Professional Experience

  • Oracle Labs

    Zürich, Switzerland

    Research Intern

    July – November 2021
    • Implemented an optimization phase in the core of the GraalVM compiler. It performs “strip mining” on long-running loops in Java programs, transforming loops into nested loops and moving overhead-heavy operations out of the inner loop.
    • This reduces the execution cost of safepoint polling for garbage collection and improves performance with low-pause-time garbage collectors. It also enables further optimizations, such as vectorization, that combine for large speed-ups.
